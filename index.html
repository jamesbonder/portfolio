About me

Front-End Web Developer based in the UK. I apply knowledge and skills of the latest standards and technologies to create responsive, performance driven websites that work cross-platform and on a wide range of devices and form factors.

Technologies I use include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Git, SASS/LESS and Wordpress. As a designer, I use tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator alongside technologies such as SVG to bring design to life on the web.

When i'm not writing code or designing websites, I enjoy running, playing guitar, video editing and playing with mechanical keyboards.
